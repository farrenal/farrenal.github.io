What is this?

Here you will find an interactive gallery where you can pick the system temperature \(T\) and magnetic field strength \(H\) of the \(q\)-Potts model for different values of \(q\), which is the number of states available to a lattice site.

Over our 2022 Christmas break Mikey Whelan, Luke Hodgkiss and I independently studied Prof. Tong's Part III notes on statistical field theory. Alongside completing problem sheet 1 and some of 2, we learned about mean field theory, path integrals and renormalisation groups. I found the concept of criticality interesting so I proposed to study in more detail the behaviour of the Potts model and came up with this fun way of presenting the topic.

See our report for an explanation of what you are seeing below. (Visuals take a moment to load.)
