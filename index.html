Research Experience

Received the Naughton Fellowship to do an REU at the University of Notre Dame for the Summer 2023. I worked with Prof. Ragnar Stroberg on spurious isospin symmetry breaking in the IMSRG.

See report and presentation.

Electoral Redistricting

Sparked by the Real World Mathematical Modelling event in May 2023, applied multi-objective optimisation and data science techniques to the elecotral redistricting of Ireland. I worked with Eliza Somerville, Ben McGloin and Mikey Whelan.

See report and code.

Physics Communication

Wrote and filmed a video series introducing index notation, the Levi-Civita symbol and Einstein summation convention for the Theoretical Physics Students Association (TPSA) Youtube channel. Recently created a video on deriving the critical temperature of the 2D Ising Model with Mikey Whelan.
